## Channel mix (the three plays)

Distribution runs on three plays, all organic-first and on-brand (no shame hooks, no fake urgency, respect the communities):

1. **Intent capture** — get in front of people *already* looking for a fix: SEO / long-tail ("focus app for ADHD on Mac," "body doubling app," "always-in-view task timer"), app-store/marketplace search, and answering high-intent questions in ADHD forums/Reddit. Captures existing demand. _Status: TBD._
2. **Audience borrowing** — borrow trust from people who already have the audience. **This is the creator program** (below); podcast guesting + newsletter placements later. _Status: active — creator pilot._
3. **Guerrilla marketing** — unconventional, low-cost, high-creativity moments: authentic presence + build-in-public in ADHD communities, relatable clips/memes, founder-story stunts. _Status: TBD._

## Creator Program (primary motion)

Organic, creator-led distribution to ADHD micro/mid creators. North Star is **activations + paid conversions, not views.** Outreach templates, one-pager, talking points, and the do/don't card live in `gtm-creator-kit.md`. Candidate creators + pilot cohort live in `gtm-creator-targets.md`.

**Target — a creator qualifies only if:**
- Makes ADHD / focus / "how I work" content (not generic productivity).
- Has lived ADHD experience (authenticity is the product).
- Audience skews knowledge workers on a computer (ICP #1), not just students on phones.
- High engagement + positive comment sentiment (read comments, not follower count).
- Not saturated with sponsorships.
- Size: micro/mid (~10k–250k) over mega.

**Platforms:** YouTube primary (desktop tool needs to be *seen* — always-in-view, parking lot, check-ins; "how I focus with ADHD" long-form converts + lives in search). TikTok / IG Reels for reach and the "aha" clip.

**Offer (performance-aligned, low-cash):**
- Gift lifetime access first, no strings → partnership only if they genuinely like it (two-step).
- Affiliate / bounty per paid conversion (keep it simple — one model).
- Audience offer via unique code = **extended free trial (30 days proposed)**, not a discount (on-brand, no fake urgency, doubles as tracking).
- Flat fees only in phase 2, for creators who *proved* conversion.

**Brief:** talking points, never a script. Lead with the creator's real workflow; show the integration moat (one window replacing five tools), not "always on top." Founder story is fair game.
- ❌ No "clinically validated / expert-reviewed / treats ADHD" (review-board gated).
- ❌ No shame hooks, "distracted minds," or fake urgency.
- ✅ Must disclose `#ad` / `#sponsored` / `#gifted` (FTC).

**Tracking:** unique promo code per creator (codes beat links for a download product). Funnel: trial → activation (first completed focus session) → paid. Judge creators on activation + paid.

**Timeline:** W1 build kit + tracking + target list (~25). W2 outreach to 20–30, gift access. W3–4 pilot posts, measure per code. W5+ double down on winners, cut non-converters, expand.

## Launch sequencing

Pre-launch → launch → sustain. First paid-attention motion: **creator pilot (ICP #1), Weeks 1–5** (see Creator Program). Product Hunt (Tier 1) and Show HN (ICP #2) = timed spikes, later.

## Metrics

- **North Star:** paid conversions from the creator funnel (trial → activation → paid). Interim signal: **activations** (first completed focus session).
- **Guardrail:** activation rate per creator/code — a creator driving trials that don't activate is a *bad fit*, not a win. Cut, don't scale.
